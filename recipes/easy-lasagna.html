Easy Lasagna

Description

A simple recipe for making tasty lasagna quickly.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
    • Cottage cheese
    • Mozzarella
    • Parmesan
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
